Welcome To Halo History

What is this site?

This website is created for the purpose of educating gamers of the massive game franchise Halo. On this site you will find an interactive timeline showing you the history of the franchise that spans 20 years! As well as an interactive timeline, we also have a newsletter. If you like what you find and want to know more about Halo, our newsletter is the place. You will recieve weekly lore information and fun facts! With 20 years of history there are months, if not years worth of information and deep story to share.

What is Halo?

Halo is a legendary gaming franchise that began all the way back in 2001. In the begining it was a simple console fps (first-person shooter). However despite its simplicity it would redefine what it meant to be a shooter on console. With industry defining controls, open enviroments and addictive couch co-op it became a must have game almost immediately. Back then it was only available for Microsoft's console the 'Xbox' however 20 years later and it would have seen release on mobile, pc and would even see movie and TV content. Want to know more?
